When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (email, username, subscription details)
  • All audio recordings
  • All transcriptions
  • All AI-generated summaries
  • Usage history and statistics

Method 1: Delete In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Recaply app on your device
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your choice

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

What to include in your email:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Confirmation that you understand this is permanent

Response time: We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email once complete.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email
  • You will no longer be able to log in to your account
  • Any active subscriptions will be cancelled
  • This action cannot be reversed

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted immediately from our active systems
  • Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days
  • Some anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytics
  • Transaction records may be retained as required by law
