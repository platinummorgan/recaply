Privacy Policy for Recaply

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Introduction

Recaply ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Account authentication tokens
  • Subscription tier information (Free, Lite, Pro)
  • Usage metrics (minutes used for transcription)

Audio and Transcription Data

  • Audio recordings you create within the app
  • Transcriptions of your audio recordings
  • AI-generated summaries of your transcriptions
  • Recording metadata (filename, duration, file size, creation date)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • App usage statistics
  • Error logs and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide audio transcription and summarization services
  • Maintain your account and manage subscriptions
  • Track usage limits based on your subscription tier
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

OpenAI (Whisper API & GPT)

  • Purpose: Audio transcription and text summarization
  • Data Shared: Audio recordings and transcribed text
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy/

Supabase

  • Purpose: Database, authentication, and file storage
  • Data Shared: User accounts, audio files, transcriptions
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Expo

  • Purpose: Mobile app development and updates
  • Data Shared: App analytics and crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

Data Storage and Security

  • Audio files and transcriptions are stored securely on Supabase cloud storage
  • All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Authentication uses industry-standard JWT tokens
  • We retain your data only as long as your account is active

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your transcriptions and summaries
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: Upon account deletion, all personal data, recordings, and transcriptions are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 2 years may be automatically deleted

Children's Privacy

Recaply is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for material changes

Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Recaply app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your choice

Alternatively, email us at support@recaply.app to request account deletion.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Compliance

We comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines

By using Recaply, you agree to this privacy policy.
